Hi there!

My name is George Stamoulis and I am the developer of Xenoglossy. This is my homepage and this is Xenoglossy's. Both are probably under heavy construction as you may have noticed, and both are probably going to stay that way for the foreseable future!

Although Xenoglossy does, upon request, change the locale for the whole site, it only manages the translation of the posts. It will not manage the translation of menus, widgets, plugins etc. This is done by the gettext files. For a better understanding have a look here. Xenoglossy itself though, is translation ready.
